# International-Maritime-Logistics-Dashboard
## 📋 Project Overview

This Power BI project delivers a comprehensive, real-time view of **international maritime logistics**. It tracks vessel movements, container volumes, durations, delays, bottlenecks, and regional performance across the globe.

The dashboard is built to help logistics professionals, shipping companies, and port operators:
- Monitor **duration time required for vessels** end-to-end
- Analyze the **2021 Suez Canal blockage** and its ripple effects on global trade
- Evaluate **terminal utilization and efficiency**
- Identify under-performing terminals and vessels instantly

**Main metrics tracked:**
- Total Movements & Containers
- On-Time / Delayed / Bottleneck percentages
- Average Duration (days)
- Regional & Vessel-type breakdowns
- Terminal volume & utilization

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Fully interactive slicers** (Year, Quarter, Region, Movement Type)
- **Dynamic KPIs** at a glance (8M containers, 15K movements, 21-day avg. duration, etc.)
- **Trend analysis** over time with weekly movement charts
- **Performance breakdowns** by region (EMEA, APAC, AMER, LATAM), vessel type, and terminal
- **Suez Canal Block deep-dive** with pre/during/post metrics
- **Terminal efficiency matrix** (utilization vs. duration scatter + day/night comparison)
- **Bottom 10 performers** table with color-coded risk indicators
- Clean, professional maritime-themed design

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. International Maritime Logistics Dashboard (Overview)
High-level KPIs, weekly trend line, performance pie chart, regional bar charts, vessel-type bottlenecks, and bottom-10 terminal/vessel ranking.

### 2. Suez Canal Block Analysis (23 Mar – 29 Mar 2021)
Dedicated page showing the impact of the **Ever Given** blockage:
- Pre-Suez vs. Suez Week vs. Post-Suez metrics
- Movement distribution by region during the crisis (EMEA hit hardest)
- Duration trends with highlighted blockage period
- Total impact: 175 movements analyzed

### 3. Terminal Efficiency Analysis
- Top/busy terminals by container volume
- Utilization % vs. average duration scatter plot
- Duration distribution by vessel category
- Day vs. Night performance comparison
- Under-utilized vs. over-utilized terminals
